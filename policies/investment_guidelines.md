# Investment Guidelines & Compliance Policies

## Fund: Master Fund LP
## Effective Date: January 1, 2024
## Version: 3.2

---

## 1. Position Limits

### 1.1 Gross Exposure
- **Maximum Gross Exposure**: 200% of NAV
- **Warning Threshold**: 180% of NAV
- **Calculation**: (Long Market Value + |Short Market Value|) / NAV
- **Monitoring Frequency**: Daily, intraday during volatile markets

### 1.2 Net Exposure
- **Maximum Net Long**: 100% of NAV
- **Maximum Net Short**: -50% of NAV
- **Warning Threshold**: 90% long, -40% short
- **Calculation**: (Long MV - |Short MV|) / NAV

### 1.3 Single Position Limits
- **Maximum Single Long**: 10% of NAV
- **Maximum Single Short**: 5% of NAV
- **Excludes**: Index hedges, ETFs used for hedging

---

## 2. Concentration Limits

### 2.1 Sector Concentration
- **Maximum Sector Exposure**: 30% of NAV
- **Sectors**: GICS Level 1 classification
- **Exceptions**: Technology sector permitted up to 40% with CIO approval

### 2.2 Geographic Concentration
- **Maximum Single Country**: 50% of NAV (excluding US)
- **Emerging Markets Total**: 25% of NAV maximum
- **Countries**: Based on issuer domicile

### 2.3 Issuer Concentration
- **Maximum Single Issuer**: 10% of NAV
- **Definition**: All securities of a single corporate family
- **Government Bonds**: Excluded from issuer limits

---

## 3. Liquidity Requirements

### 3.1 Liquidity Buckets
| Bucket | Timeframe | Minimum |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| T+1 | Same/next day | 10% NAV |
| T+3 | Within 3 days | 25% NAV |
| T+7 | Within 1 week | 40% NAV |
| T+30 | Within 1 month | 60% NAV |
| T+90 | Within 1 quarter | 80% NAV |

### 3.2 Liquidity Calculation
- Based on 20-day average daily volume (ADV)
- Position liquidation assumes 25% of ADV participation
- Mark-to-market with 10% slippage for illiquid positions

### 3.3 Cash Management
- **Minimum Cash Buffer**: 2% of NAV
- **Maximum Cash**: 15% of NAV (opportunity cost)

---

## 4. Counterparty Exposure

### 4.1 Prime Broker Limits
- **Maximum Single Prime**: 50% of NAV
- **Minimum Primes**: 2 active prime relationships
- **Credit Quality**: Investment grade required (BBB- or higher)

### 4.2 OTC Counterparty Limits
- **Maximum Single Counterparty**: 5% of NAV (net exposure)
- **Collateral Requirements**: Daily margin with threshold $0
- **Netting Agreements**: ISDA required for all OTC trades

### 4.3 Counterparty Monitoring
- Daily mark-to-market of all OTC positions
- Weekly credit review for material exposures
- Immediate notification of rating downgrades

---

## 5. Prohibited Activities

### 5.1 Absolute Prohibitions
- No physical commodities ownership
- No direct real estate ownership
- No lending of fund assets outside securities lending program
- No investments in tobacco or controversial weapons manufacturers

### 5.2 Restricted Activities (Require Board Approval)
- Private placements > 10% of NAV
- Investments with lock-up > 1 year
- Co-investments with GP or affiliates

---

## 6. Risk Metrics

### 6.1 Value-at-Risk (VaR)
- **Confidence Level**: 99%
- **Time Horizon**: 1 day
- **Maximum VaR**: 3% of NAV
- **Methodology**: Historical simulation, 2-year lookback

### 6.2 Stress Testing
- Weekly stress tests required
- Scenarios: 2008 GFC, COVID-19, Rising rates
- Maximum drawdown in any scenario: 15% of NAV

---

## 7. NAV Reconciliation

### 7.1 Daily Reconciliation
- Internal NAV vs Administrator NAV
- **Tolerance**: 0.10% (10 basis points)
- **Escalation**: CCO notification if tolerance exceeded

### 7.2 Position Reconciliation
- Positions reconciled to prime brokers daily
- Cash reconciled to banks daily
- OTC positions marked independently

---

## 8. Exception Management

### 8.1 Breach Classification
| Severity | Definition | Response Time |
|----------|------------|---------------|
| Critical | >10% over limit | Immediate |
| Major | 5-10% over limit | Same day |
| Minor | <5% over limit | 48 hours |

### 8.2 Escalation Matrix
1. **Level 1**: Portfolio Manager notification
2. **Level 2**: CIO notification (if not cured in 24h)
3. **Level 3**: CCO and Board notification (if not cured in 48h)
4. **Level 4**: Investor notification (if material and ongoing)

### 8.3 Documentation Requirements
- All breaches logged with timestamp
- Root cause analysis required within 5 business days
- Remediation plan documented
- CCO sign-off on closure

---

## 9. Reporting Requirements

### 9.1 Daily Reports
- Position summary
- Exposure summary
- Limit utilization dashboard

### 9.2 Weekly Reports
- Stress test results
- Liquidity analysis

### 9.3 Monthly Reports

- Control testing results
